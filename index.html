The main sources of profitability

01

Algorithmic trading

Using automated trading systems to make transactions in the crypto market. Our algorithms analyze the market and execute trades, providing stable income even in high volatility conditions.

02

Staking

Proof-of-Stake (PoS) allows you to earn passive income by confirming transactions on the blockchain. Popular tokens for staking include Ethereum (ETH), Cosmos Hub (ATOM), Tezos (XTZ), and Cardano (ADA).

03

Yield Farming

Provide liquidity to decentralized exchanges (DEXs) and receive a portion of the commissions. Yield farming offers high rewards but comes with risks of volatility and volatile losses.

04

Cloud Mining

Cloud mining simplifies the process of mining cryptocurrencies by renting computing power. This reduces capital expenditure and allows you to generate income from mining without the need to own equipment.

05

Crypto Interest Accounts

Earn interest on cryptocurrency deposits with flexible and fixed terms. Interest rates are higher than traditional banks, making this an attractive option for long-term investments.

06

Lending

Lend crypto loans to generate income. P2P lending, centralized and decentralized lending, and margin lending are available.
